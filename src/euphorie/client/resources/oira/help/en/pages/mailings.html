Mailings

Keep updated by signing up for our newsletters.

To stay updated with the latest news and developments and possible OSH information for your sector, you can subscribe to our newsletters. You can choose between different newsletters (on tool, country and EU level). Follow this step-by-step instruction to manage your subscriptions:

Accessing the preferences

  1. To access the settings for mailings, you need to get to the ‘Preferences’ screen:
  2. Look at the top right corner of the OiRA homepage; you will find the personal menu icon. Click on this icon.
  3. A dropdown menu will appear. In this menu, locate and select ‘Preferences’.

Once you are on the ‘Preferences’ screen, look for the ‘Mailings’ section. This is where you can tick the boxes for subscribing to the newsletters you are interested in. Specific sectoral tool newsletters only show here if you have already started a risk assessment with the tool.

Managing your subscriptions

  1. Remember, all subscriptions are optional with the exception of functional e-mails. Functional e-mails are necessary for using your OiRA account, e.g. they can be linked to password resets, so they cannot be turned off.
  2. To subscribe to a newsletter, you will see a list of potential newsletters based on the tools which your organisation has used at least once. Choose which tool-based newsletters you want to receive by clicking on the checkboxes beside them.
  3. Additionally, you can opt in for newsletters specific to your sector level, country level and EU level.

After you have made your choices, do not forget to click on the ‘Save’ button on the toolbar to ensure your preferences are saved.

You can return to the ‘Preferences’ screen and adjust your settings anytime you want to make changes to your subscriptions.

It is recommended to periodically review your newsletter subscriptions and adjust them according to your preferences.
